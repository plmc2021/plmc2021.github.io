    PHILIPPINE LEGACY MOTOCLUB

    Sec # 2021050013730

    The organization shall be known PHILIPPINE LEGACY MOTOCLUB. This club was founded and organized on March 28,2021 in Statue ni david Davao City and is under the authority of the Philippines.

    VISSION

    To help each rider in times of life and death situation, crisis, and in any way help is needed which agrees to the standards and conformities of this rider organization.

    MISSION

    To recruit more members inclined with Rider's club and so we can help and reach out to them as main purpose of this organization.

    GOAL

    Is to create a welcoming community for all motorcycle, enthusiasts of all levels and backgrounds to share their passion for riding to promote a safe and inclusive event where members can enjoy charity rides, social events, and skill-building workshops.

    SECTION I

    To apply for membership of Philippine Legacy MotoClub, you must meet the following criteria:
    A.You are of legal age
    B.You are interested in the sport of motorcycling and the promotion of safe motorcycling and can strictly follow the rules established by this club The officers of this club will assess and evaluate all rider‚Äôs application to make sure criteria are met and will be notified if the membership application is accepted or denied.

    SECTION II

    All events/ rides must be facilitated by admin officer/s with the help of its officials.

    SECTION III

    To give proper preparation, all events/rides must be posted at leat 2 weeks prior to the date of the ride /event.

    SECTION IV

    Charity and events shall be properly prepared, organised and facilitated and shall be coordinated to the team; a report must be submitted.

    SECTION V

    Important, special and urgent meetings can be called anytime by the President of the Club.

    SECTION VI

    Time and place will be decided by the officers and will be cascaded to every members of the club.
